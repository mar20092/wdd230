Target Audience Description:

The audience will be people living in Argentina, that are interested in receving professional advise for building their business.

Person 1:

David. He's 31 years old, married and has three little kids. He have saved from money since he was single, and being that is not possible for him to buy or build a house for his family, he decided to start a business.

Person 2:

Jessica. She's 38 years old, single and has no kids. She is an active business woman who is decided to start a business in Argentina, but has no experience with South America, and specially Argentina rules and laws. She is looking to receive benefits for being part of the local chamber of commerce.

Scenarios:

1. A person wants to know the benefits of being part of the local chamber of commerce.

2. A person wants to know the current laws and rules to start a business in Argentina.

3. A person wants to receive advise on running a business without taking many risks.

4. A customer received an unfair treat from a local business and wants to know where to find help.

5. A person concerned about the changing economy wants to know if it's safe to invest in his friend local business.
