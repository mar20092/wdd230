Target Audience Description:

The audience will be people living in Argentina, that are interested in getting the weather in real-time. They also are worried about climate change and try to find answers to the last climate changes visible in our days.

Person 1:

David. He's 31 years old, married and has three little kids. He checks the weather every time he has to go out, especially if goes with his kids. He's also worried about their future, how the climate change will affect the lives of his children, and wants to be updated about the changes that goverments and companies are making to stop the climate change. He likes to receive news on his email address.

Person 2:

Jessica. She's 38 years old, single and has no kids. She is active in every event were climate change is being discussed. She is very commited with the enviroment and understand that people need to change the way they produce and consume energy. She likes outside exercices and always check the clima before going out to run.

Scenarios:

1. A person wants to know the weather to see what clothing needs to put on for running outside.

2. A person wants to know the weather to see what clothing his/her children need to have when going to school.

3. A person wants to see the weather prognostic for the next weekend to plan a picnic.

4. A person militating against climate change wants to know the last news about it.

5. A person concerned about climate change wants to read more information about the topic.
